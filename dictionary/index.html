Sound

To be sound: Irish slang. To be sound is a state of existence. It usually refers to someone who willingly does a favour, asked or not, although usually unexpected - Like when you ask for one [rollie] and your given two, or the [bouncer] to an over 21s let's you in when [your 20].\n\nAlternatively, \"sound\" can be used as an adjective to describe a genuine, nice person.
